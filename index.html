Converged Platforms (HCDC)

Converged Platforms (HCDC) is a dynamic distributor of IT infrastructure software, representing the convergence of technology, innovation, and strategic partnership. Founded in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, the company serves as a regional hub, connecting global vendors with fast-growing markets across Central Asia and the Middle East — including Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Armenia, Israel, and the UAE. Our focus is on enabling enterprises with robust, scalable, and secure IT infrastructure solutions that empower digital transformation and sustainable growth.
